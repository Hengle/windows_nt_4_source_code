Cyberspace Sampler

The starting points below are a scant sampling of the enormous and ever-growing volume of information on the Internet, but they will help acquaint you with both Mosaic and the World Wide Web. Of course, you need an Internet connection for these links to work.

Hints for exploring:

  • To stop a file transfer, press Escape on your keyboard or click the Stop button in the toolbar.

  • When you find an interesting file, you can choose Add Current to Hotlist from the Navigate menu to add it to your hotlist.

  • If you cannot retrieve a particular file, try again later. The server may be overloaded.

  • If you become lost and want to return to a familiar starting point, choose Home from the Navigate menu or click on the Home button in the toolbar.
